# 🎛️ DJ Mixer — v2.0.0 "Field Unit"

**A full two-deck DJ console that runs entirely in your browser — now with a memory, a crate-digger, and a brand-new look.** Load YouTube links, audio URLs, your own files, or built-in beat loops. Real waveforms, real beatmatching, and a library that remembers everything you play. No installs, no logins, no plugins.

▶ **Try it now:** https://sudockin.github.io/dj-mixer/

---

## 🆕 What's new since v1.0

### 🎨 A whole new look — "field unit" hardware console
Out with the neon; in with a light industrial console: warm-gray desk, off-white machine panels, dark LCD screens, charcoal keycaps, fader-cap sliders, and glowing LED status badges. Type set in **Archivo** + **IBM Plex Mono**. Same app, dressed for the road.

### 🎧 Real mixing controls
- **Cue** — set & jump, with a marker on the waveform
- **Loops** — 1 / 2 / 4 / 8 bars, beat-perfect and sync-safe
- **Quantize** — snap cues & loops to the beat
- **Crossfader curves** — Smooth (equal-power), Linear, Cut

### 🤖 Auto-sync
Hit play on a second deck while one's running and — with **Auto-sync** on — it locks to the master's tempo *and* phase automatically, holding within a few milliseconds. No headphones, no guesswork.

### 📚 Music Library (it remembers)
Every track you load is **saved automatically** with title, artist, source, duration, BPM, date, and play count. Browse **Recently Loaded**, **Favorites ❤️**, and date-grouped **History**, **search** instantly, and reload to either deck in one click. **Drag & drop** files or links straight onto a deck.

### 📋 Queue
"**Queue next**" from the library, reorder by drag or ▲/▼, and let **auto-advance** roll the next track when a deck ends. Now Playing + Up Next at a glance.

### 🔎 Discover (crate-digging via Discogs)
For any track, pull genre / style / year / label and get **"more like this"** and **"more by this artist."** Send a pick **straight to a deck** (opens a YouTube search — or one-click loads with your own YouTube API key).

## 🧠 Still the core: beatmatching that holds
SYNC with continuous **phase-lock**, a **beat grid** on the waveform, a **drift meter**, and manual **nudge** — verified to hold two tracks aligned within milliseconds for minutes, not seconds.

## 📱 Works on your phone
Landscape layout keeps both decks + crossfader on screen, touch-friendly controls, iOS audio handling, safe-area support, and a rotate prompt in portrait.

## ⌨️ Shortcuts
`Q`/`W` nudge A · `O`/`P` nudge B · `Z`/`M` jump to cue · `G` quantize · `?` shortcuts panel
